Market Opportunity: The Memory Super-Cycle

The advent of Artificial Intelligence is driving an unprecedented demand for high-performance memory, ushering in a new memory super-cycle. Micron is strategically positioned to benefit from this structural shift, with significant growth opportunities across various segments.

Key Market Drivers

  • Exponential growth in AI inference and training demand.
  • Massive transition from traditional servers to AI-optimized servers.
  • Increasing memory content per server and per device.
  • Emergence of new AI applications across industries.

Demand-Supply Dynamics & Pricing Power

  • Rational supply management by leading memory manufacturers.
  • Tight supply of advanced HBM and DDR5, leading to favorable pricing.
  • Long-term contracts and strong customer relationships.
  • Micron's ability to command premium pricing for its leading-edge products.
