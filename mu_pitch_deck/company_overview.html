Company Overview: A Global Leader in Memory Solutions

Micron Technology is a world leader in innovative memory and storage solutions, transforming how the world uses information to enrich life. With a rich history of innovation, Micron is at the forefront of technological advancements critical for data-intensive applications like Artificial Intelligence.

Key Products

  • DRAM (Dynamic Random-Access Memory): Essential for computing, including High Bandwidth Memory (HBM) for AI and DDR5 for servers.
  • NAND Flash: Non-volatile storage solutions, including Solid-State Drives (SSDs) and Universal Flash Storage (UFS).
  • NOR Flash: High-performance, reliable memory for embedded applications.

Market Position & Competitive Landscape

  • One of only three major DRAM manufacturers globally, alongside Samsung and SK Hynix.
  • Strong position in the high-growth HBM market, critical for AI accelerators.
  • Diversified product portfolio serving computing, mobile, automotive, and industrial markets.
  • Continuous innovation in process technology and product development.
